STAR WARS

match match game

The player turns over two cards, one at a time. The cards are kept in position. If the cards do not match, the player turns the cards face-down. Play continues until all the cards have been picked up. You should watch carefully and remember where cards are located. Then, when you turn over your first card, you may remember where to find a matching card.
